WELCOME TO THE CAMPAIGN

The Police, Crime, Courts and Sentencing Bill, currently being debated in UK Parliament, aims to limit our fundamental freedoms regarding protest.

If this bill comes to pass, everyone living in the UK can expect to see strict limits placed on their rights to protest and freedom of assembly and expression. This includes the grounds on which a police officer can arrest someone participating in a protest, and the type of sentence an arrested protester is likely to receive. Most importantly, many decisions about the type of conduct that is cause for the arrest of a protestor, may be made by the police themselves.

WHAT CAN WE DO ABOUT IT?

We want you to let your MPs know that this bill must be stopped.

This letter writing campaign aims to let MPs know that there is significant concern about what's contained in this bill. You can participate by sending a letter to your MP. If you live in England or Wales, you can use the England & Wales letter template. If you live in Scotland, you can use the Scotland Letter Template. You can find out who your MP is by entering your postcode here.
